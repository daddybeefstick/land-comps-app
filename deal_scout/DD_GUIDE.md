# Land Subdivision Due Diligence Checklist

Use this when reviewing properties from your Deal Scout sheet. Order varies by situation.

---

## 1. Zillow listing (use "View on Zillow" link)

- **Description** – Read "What's special" / "Overview" for hints on zoning, access, improvements, issues
- **Photos** – Aerial shots, frontage, structures; look for wetlands, flood zones, access
- **Days on market** – High DOM may mean overpriced or problems
- **Agent/MLS#** – Note for title/parcel lookup

---

## 2. Parcel & title

- **Parcel ID** – Use "Lookup Parcel" link or county GIS/assessor; get APN (Assessor Parcel Number)
- **Deed** – County deed records or recorder of deeds
- **Title** – Liens, easements, judgments; consider a title search/insurance
- **Ownership** – Confirm current owner matches listing

---

## 3. Zoning & subdivision rules

- **Zoning** – County/city zoning map; confirm residential or subdividable
- **Minimum lot size** – Required for new lots (e.g. 1 acre, 2 acres)
- **Subdivision regulations** – Road standards, utility requirements, plat approval

---

## 4. Access & utilities

- **Road frontage** – Deeded access, recorded easement, or landlocked?
- **Utilities** – Power, water, sewer (or septic); cost to extend vs. onsite
- **Culverts/driveways** – Wet crossings, permits, maintenance

---

## 5. Physical/Environmental

- **FEMA flood map** – [msc.fema.gov](https://msc.fema.gov) – zone A/VE = high risk
- **Wetlands** – Army Corps jurisdictional; impacts developable area
- **Soil** – Perc test for septic; shrink-swell, rock for building
- **Topo/slope** – Drainage and buildable area

---

## 6. Costs & numbers

- **Taxes** – Current tax bill; reassessment after sale
- **Survey** – Existing vs. new; cost to subdivide
- **Infra** – Road, utilities, grading per lot
- **Holding** – Loan, taxes, insurance until lots sold

---

## 7. Exit (sell-through)

- **Comparables** – Recent lot sales in the area
- **Absorption** – How fast lots sell (Zillow County Sales data helps here)
- **Price per acre** – Market vs. your buy + costs

---

## Red flags (from your sheet)

- Flood/wetland mention
- HOA, deed restrictions, landlocked, no access
- Long DOM + no price cut
- ROI below ~25% (rough screening)

---

## Quick order

1. Zillow → overview
2. Parcel lookup → ID and county records
3. Zoning → can you subdivide?
4. Access & utilities → how much to build?
5. Flood & env → buildable area?
6. Numbers → deal works?
